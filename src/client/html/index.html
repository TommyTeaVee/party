Spatial Video Chat.

made with by @souramoo :)

Most video call apps are designed for conferences, not fun.

Normally only one person can speak at a time in a standard conference call and it only gets worse the more people there are.
This isn't like in real life, where we zone in and out of large groups, form smaller ones as the conversation takes us.


What if we can do better?

Let's make better use of our virtual space.
Dip in and out of groups by walking around just like a real party.
Neural networks turn facial expressions into emojis to communicate what people are doing from a distance.

You don't even need to log in, all you need is your face.

This is all a bit of an experiment, why not try it out?
